A temporary exemption for a period of time not exceeding more than 5 years may be granted under this paragraph (b)(4). An exemption under this paragraph (b)(4) must be approved by the Council on Development Finance and the Director of Small Business under Â§Â§ 8705A and 8707A of Title 29 and Â§ 5178 of this title. An exemption under this paragraph (b)(4) may not be approved and must be revoked by the Council on Development Finance and the Director of Small Business to any commercial airline that transports individuals detained by the U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement for the purpose of deportation without one or more of the following: a.
