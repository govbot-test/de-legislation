SYNOPSIS

This Amendment to House Bill No. 294 provides that the Director of the Office of Management and Budget must designate 1 or more reserved parking spaces for pregnant persons at certain designated state buildings and facilities if the State owns those locations. If the State leases the location, the Director is encouraged to work with the lessor to designate the reserved parking space.

This Amendment also directs the Director of the Office of Management and Budget to consider the designation of reserved parking spaces for pregnant persons at future State-owned and leased facilities where feasible.
