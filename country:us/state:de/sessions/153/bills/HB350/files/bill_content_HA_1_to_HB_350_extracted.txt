Legislation Document SPONSOR: Rep.Â Neal HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY HOUSE AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO HOUSE BILL NO. 350 AMEND House Bill No. 350 on line 35 by deleting "and " after the comma and before "vocational" therein.
